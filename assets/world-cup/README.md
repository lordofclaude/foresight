# Foresight international-football image set

Ten original raster assets generated with the built-in image-generation tool on 2026-07-18. The set deliberately avoids official FIFA marks, sponsor signage, recognizable player likenesses, and replicas of a real tournament trophy.

All project files are WebP at quality 84. Combined transfer size is about 1.62 MB; individual files range from about 83 KB to 279 KB.

| Asset | Primary prompt | Product role |
|---|---|---|
| `championship-trophy-hero.webp` | Original faceted-globe championship cup above a night stadium; gold, navy, lime/cyan accents; left-side negative space. | Pitch, presentation, or campaign hero. |
| `stadium-night-panorama.webp` | Monumental floodlit stadium immediately before kickoff; symmetrical pitch; dark overlay-safe edges. | Selected-match hero background. |
| `supporters-celebration.webp` | International supporters celebrating a late goal beneath confetti; silhouettes; colorful world-stage energy. | Community/social campaign asset. |
| `goal-net-impact.webp` | Ball striking a rain-soaked goal net at the instant of impact; decisive and kinetic. | Outcome/settlement campaign asset. |
| `tactical-data-pitch.webp` | Top-down tactical pitch fused with passing lanes, heat zones, nodes, and probability pulses. | Strategy/agent campaign asset. |
| `golden-boot-strike.webp` | Unbranded gold boot striking a ball on wet turf under floodlights; high-speed impact. | Market-move campaign asset. |

Shared style prompt: premium cinematic international-football imagery, midnight navy foundation, electric lime and cyan, restrained magenta and warm gold, no text, no logos, no watermark, no official tournament branding.

## Placement rules

- The landing gate and selected-match hero use optimized CSS backgrounds with strong dark gradients so text remains legible.
- The data workspace intentionally does not load an image carousel. The remaining eight files are reserved for pitch, presentation, receipt-card, and campaign use.
- Reduced-data removes both decorative dashboard backgrounds.
- These images provide atmosphere only. They never indicate live state, proof status, or evidence authority.
