THE FIVE LAYERS: L1 commit hash + external timestamp — proven for real: two genuine commits are anchored on Solana devnet, including a World Cup FINAL pick anchored ~19h BEFORE kickoff (see the ⛓ card; txs verifiable on the explorer) — every other commit in this app is a local sim-slot (not yet individually broadcast on-chain — see below) · L2 anchored price at commit (/api/odds/validation) · L3 reveal-or-burn · L4 grading reads the game_finalised statKeys 1001+3001 / 1002+3002 (90' 1X2) — prod CPIs validateStatV2, the SettleKit binding pattern minus the vault · L5 $100-flat at de-vigged anchored prices + shrinkage; earliness receipts gated at 120s (measured ±105s feed-noise band). REAL WALLET MODE: only a future fixture or a verified actively streaming fixture may sign a Solana memo. Completed and captured historical fixtures always remain local practice, even with a wallet connected. SIGN-IN & WHAT'S STILL SIMULATED: Google + Solana sign-in on the landing gate is real (Clerk-powered — Google OAuth + Sign-in-with-Solana); Polymarket links open their real site for you to trade — Foresight can't execute for you; premium follow unlock has no payment processor (explicitly simulated, never a silent charge).
